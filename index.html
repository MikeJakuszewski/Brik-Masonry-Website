About Us

With a rich history spanning over 25 years, Brik Masonry has established itself as a premier builder of bespoke homes in the Greater Toronto Area. Our team specializes in creating stunning and structurally sound custom projects that perfectly suit the needs and preferences of our clients. Our services range from Custom Masonry, Brick and Stone work, Chimneys, Flagstone, Hardscaping, and more, ensuring that we have the expertise to bring any design vision to life.

Services

Our masonry services include custom designs, brick and stone work, chimneys, flagstone, hardscaping, and more. Trust us to bring your vision to life with our experienced team and high-quality craftsmanship.
